Know Margaret Better

Margaret is a committed servant leader driven by a strong passion to serve humanity and make the world a better place for everyone to live in.
She is a visionary, transformative, results driven high school principal with a degree in Master of Education Leadership and Management. She possess strong religious moral values which are anchored on the teachings found in the Bible. Whatever she does, she works at it with all her heart, as though working for the Lord. Margaret is a trained in Child Protection and is committed to protecting all children, all the time, in any part of the world.
“And whatever my hands find to do, I do it with the whole of my heart.”

Margaret Kangai Kirimu has been working with the Teachers Service Commission for 30 years. She is a progressive, results driven high school principal. She is also a renown defender of Children’s Rights. She is committed in assisting the needy and vulnerable students access education and realize their potential. Besides, she is a mentor and trainer in Children Protection. Margaret holds a Master Degree in Educational Leadership and Management. She also has aBachelor’s Degree in Education. She was trained in Child Protection by Gender ViolenceRecovery Centre, Nairobi in Conjunction with Teachers Service Commission. Furthermore,Margaret is a Master Trainer in Digital Literacy. She was trained by Facebook and participated in“My Digital World” digital literacy program.
