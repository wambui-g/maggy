Margaret alongside other like-minded individuals founded the Nurture Him CBO to address the unique and monumental challenges faced by the boy child in our society today. Recognizing the difficulties in transitioning into adulthood and the prevalence of issues such as crime, substance abuse, and mental health issues among boys, Margaret initiated Nurture Him with the aim of restoring dignity to boys' lives and empowering them with life and market-relevant skills. Through drug and substance abuse sensitization, rehabilitation programs, skill development, advocacy, and mentorship initiatives, Nurture Him envisions a world where every boy has the opportunity to lead a dignified life and contribute positively to society. The Program's objectives includes:

  • Raise awareness of mental health issues among boys.
  • Provide workshops to equip boys with skills to identify and cope with mental health issues.
  • Offer counseling services led by qualified professionals for personalized support.
  • Reduce stigma surrounding mental health issues through educational campaigns.
  • Promote support-seeking behaviors among young men by highlighting the importance of seeking help and providing information about available resources.
